# Homepage Specification — Alkamind

Reference designs: `HP_D.png` (desktop) · `HP_M.png` (mobile)

---

## Sections

### 1. Announcement Bar
Thin full-width green bar at very top. Short promotional text. No close button.

**Mobile:** Same, single line, smaller font.

---

### 2. Site Header
Logo centered "Alkamind". Nav left (Shop, Shop By Need, Learn). Icons right (Search, Account, Cart).

**Mobile:** Hamburger left. Logo centered. Search + Cart icons right. Nav links hidden.

---

### 3. Hero Banner
Full-width dark textured/editorial background (branded wordmark texture visible). Large display heading: "THE ONLY WAY TO GET OFF YOUR ACID". CTA button below copy.

**Mobile:** Same full-width image. Heading stacks to fewer words per line. CTA button full width.

#### Components
- **Display Heading** — 2-line large bold type.
- **CTA Button** — Primary pill/rounded button.

---

### 4. Community Strip
Subheading: "JOIN 100K+ ACID KICKERS". Horizontal scrollable strip of UGC headshot portrait photos.

**Mobile:** Same heading. Photos horizontally scrollable, same strip behavior. Slightly smaller headshots.

#### Components
- **Community Photo Strip** — Row of circular or square UGC portrait photos, auto-scroll or swipeable.

---

### 5. "You've Been Sold a Lie" Section
Large bold heading: "YOU'VE BEEN SOLD A LIE". Body copy right column. "WATCH ALL" CTA button below copy. Horizontal icon row below with symptom/category labels.

**Mobile:** Single column. Heading full width, copy below, CTA button full width. Icon row horizontally scrollable.

#### Components
- **Section Heading** — Large bold H2.
- **Body Copy** — 2–3 paragraph supporting text.
- **Watch All CTA** — Outlined or secondary button.
- **Symptom Icon Row** — Row of small icons + text labels (e.g., Acid Reflux, Bloating, Constipation, etc.). Desktop: full row visible. Mobile: horizontally scrollable.

---

### 6. Featured Products Section
Heading: "NO MORE REACHING FOR ANTACIDS OR QUICK-FIX PRESCRIPTIONS". Filter tabs above product cards (Acid Reflux & Heartburn, Brain Health, Bone Health). Horizontal row of 3 product cards. Trust badge strip below cards.

**Mobile:** Heading full width. Filter tabs horizontally scrollable. Product cards in 1-column or horizontal scroll (1 card + partial next visible). Trust badges wrap to 2-column grid or horizontal scroll.

#### Components
- **Filter Tabs** — Pill-style category tabs. Active tab highlights. Clicking swaps product card set.
- **Product Card** — Product image, category tag, name, price, variant swatches, flavor label. _Mobile: adds "ADD TO CART" button._
- **Trust Badge Strip** — Row of icon + text badges (Doctor Formulated, Organic Ingredients, Clinically Tested, Third-Party Tested, No Artificial Ingredients, Gluten-Free & Sugar-Free, Doctor Formulated, Vegan Ingredients).
  _Mobile: Horizontally scrollable strip._

---

### 7. Bundle / System Section
Split layout. Left: large lifestyle photo (woman drinking green drink). Right: "THE COMPLETE SYSTEM YOU NEED TO KICK ACID" heading, body copy, horizontal row of small product images, "BUNDLE & SAVE" primary CTA button, "SHOP BUNDLES" secondary text link.

**Mobile:** Single column stacked. Photo top full width. Text, product row, CTAs below.

#### Components
- **Lifestyle Photo** — Full-height editorial image.
- **Section Heading** — H2.
- **Body Copy** — Short supporting paragraph.
- **Product Mini Row** — Small product thumbnails with labels (inline horizontal list).
- **Primary CTA** — "BUNDLE & SAVE" pill button.
- **Secondary Link** — "SHOP BUNDLES" text link.

---

### 8. Press Logo Bar
"FEATURED IN" label. Horizontal row of publication logos: Women's Health, GQ, SI, BAZAAR, BYRDIE, The Skinny Confidant.

**Mobile:** 2–3 logos visible. Horizontally scrollable.

---

### 9. Proof Section
Split layout. Left: large editorial product/lifestyle image. Right: bold proof headline ("PROVEN TO NEUTRALIZE 99%+ OF ACID IN A VENTI STARBUCKS CUP OF COFFEE"), body copy, "SCHEDULE A CONSULT" CTA button.

**Mobile:** Single column. Image full width on top, text block below.

#### Components
- **Proof Headline** — Large bold H2 stat-driven claim.
- **Body Copy** — Supporting paragraph.
- **CTA Button** — "SCHEDULE A CONSULT" primary button.

---

### 10. Stats Bar
Heading: "WHAT HAPPENS WHEN YOU START KICKING ACID:". Four large percentage stats in a horizontal row, each with a short descriptive label below: 91%, 82%, 76%, 71%.

**Mobile:** Stats render in a 2×2 grid (2 per row).

#### Components
- **Stat Block** — Large percentage number + short descriptor label.

---

### 11. Mission Section
Dark (blue/teal) background. Left: "OUR MISSION IS PERSONAL" heading + body copy paragraphs. Right: pull quote block — quote text, founder photo, name + title attribution.

**Mobile:** Single column stacked. Heading + copy top, pull quote below. Full width.

#### Components
- **Section Heading** — H2 on dark background.
- **Body Copy** — 2–3 paragraphs.
- **Pull Quote Block** — Quote text, small founder portrait, name + title.

---

### 12. FAQ Section
Centered heading: "FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS". Accordion list of Q&A items. "SEE MORE" expand button below visible items.

**Mobile:** Full width, same accordion behavior. Larger tap targets. "SEE MORE" button full width.

#### Components
- **FAQ Accordion** — Question as header row, answer expands on click/tap.
- **See More Button** — Loads or reveals additional FAQ items.

---

### 13. Gut Check CTA Banner
Full-width editorial banner. Large display headline: "THE GUT CHECK". "FIND YOUR REFLUX TYPE" pill CTA button. Abstract/liquid full-bleed background image.

**Mobile:** Same full width. Headline stacks, slightly smaller. CTA button full width.

---

### 14. UGC / Social Gallery
"@GETOFFYOURACID" heading with social icons right-aligned (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube). Horizontal row of UGC lifestyle photo tiles.

**Mobile:** Social icons below heading, left-aligned. Photos in 2-column grid.

---

### 15. Footer
Multi-column layout. 4 nav columns (Support, Company, Explore, Reflux News You Can Use). Newsletter: email input + "SIGN UP" button. "GET OFF YOUR ACID®" large display headline. Copyright + Privacy Policy + Terms of Use bar.

**Mobile:** Newsletter signup full width at top of footer. Nav columns as accordions. Headline remains large. Legal bar stacked below.

#### Components
- **Nav Columns** — 4 grouped link lists.
  _Mobile: Accordion — tap to expand._
- **Newsletter Signup** — Email input + "SIGN UP" inline button.
  _Mobile: Full-width stacked._
- **Legal Bar** — Copyright, Privacy Policy, Terms of Use.
  _Mobile: Stacked, centered._
