# Blog Detail (Article) Specification — Alkamind

Reference designs: `Blog-Detail_D.png` (desktop) · `Blog-Detail_M.png` (mobile)

---

## Sections

### 1. Announcement Bar
Thin full-width green bar. No close button.

**Mobile:** Same.

---

### 2. Site Header
Standard. Logo centered, nav left, icons right.

**Mobile:** Hamburger left, logo center, search + cart right.

---

### 3. Article Hero
Full-width hero image. Overlaid at bottom: "Featured" badge + category tag, article title (H1), author name + date. Social share icons on right edge (vertical stack or inline).

**Mobile:** Full-width hero image. Badges, title, author + date stacked below image. Social share icons below author row (horizontal).

#### Components
- **Featured Badge** — "Featured" pill label.
- **Category Tag** — Category pill (e.g., "Acid Reflux & Heartburn").
- **Article Title** — H1, large bold.
- **Author + Date** — Author name, publish date.
- **Social Share Icons** — Share to social platforms (inline icon buttons).

---

### 4. Article Body
Constrained-width content column centered on page. Rich text article content.

**Mobile:** Full width with standard horizontal padding.

#### Components
- **Body Text** — Paragraphs of editorial copy.
- **Subheading** — H2/H3 section headers within article.
- **Inline Image** — Full-width image within article flow, with optional caption.
- **Pull Quote** — Large styled quote block with attribution.
- **Numbered / Bulleted List** — Formatted list items within article.
- **Inline Product Card** — Mid-article product recommendation. Contains: product image, name, star rating + count, variant swatches, price, "ADD TO CART" button.
  _Mobile: Full width card._

---

### 5. Comment Count
Small text below article body showing comment count (e.g., "1 comment"). Likely links to comment section.

**Mobile:** Same, full width.

---

### 6. Related Articles
"RELATED ARTICLES" heading + "SEE ALL" link right-aligned. 3-column row of article cards.

**Mobile:** Horizontal scrollable strip of article cards (1.5–2 visible at a time). Or 2-column grid.

#### Components
- **Article Card** — Cover image, category tag, min read label, title.

---

### 7. UGC / Social Gallery
"@GETOFFYOURACID" heading + social icons. Horizontal photo tile row.

**Mobile:** 2-column grid.

---

### 8. Footer
Standard footer. Nav columns, newsletter signup, "GET OFF YOUR ACID®" headline, legal bar.

**Mobile:** Newsletter full width. Nav as accordions. Legal stacked.
