# About Page Specification — Alkamind

Reference designs: `About_D.png` (desktop) · `About_M.png` (mobile)

---

## Sections

### 1. Announcement Bar
Thin full-width green bar at very top. Short promotional text ("Kick acid. Find relief."). No close button.

**Mobile:** Same, single line, smaller font.

---

### 2. Site Header
Logo centered "Alkamind". Nav left (Shop, Shop By Need, Learn). Icons right (Search, Account, Cart).

**Mobile:** Hamburger left. Logo centered. Search + Cart icons right. Nav links hidden.

---

### 3. Hero Banner
Full-width dark editorial image. "HEAL YOUR GUT" display heading centered over image.

**Mobile:** Same full-width image. Heading centered, same position. Slightly smaller type.

---

### 4. Press Logo Bar
Horizontal row of press publication logos on white background: Women's Health, GQ, SI, BAZAAR, BYRDIE, The Skinny Confidant.

**Mobile:** Horizontally scrollable. Only 2–3 logos visible at a time. Swipe to reveal rest.

---

### 5. Mission Section
Split two-column layout. Left: large portrait photo of Dr. Daryl Gioffre with name + title overlay ("DR. DARYL GIOFFRE / CEO & Co-Founder, Alkamind"). Right: "OUR ACID-KICKING MISSION" heading + body copy paragraph.

**Mobile:** Single column stacked. Heading + body copy first (full width), then full-width portrait photo with name/title overlay below.

#### Components
- **Founder Portrait** — Large photo with name + title text overlay (bottom-left of image).
- **Mission Copy Block** — H2 heading + 2–3 paragraph body text.

---

### 6. Origin Story Section
Centered H2 heading: "A PROMISE THAT STARTED IT ALL". Body copy in two-column text layout below heading. Right side: TV appearance photo (FOX5 logo visible). Left side: continuation of body copy + bold closing statement.

**Mobile:** Single column. Heading centered, body copy full width, FOX5 photo full width below copy.

#### Components
- **Section Heading** — Centered H2.
- **Body Copy** — Two-column text block (desktop). Wraps to single column on mobile.
- **Media Image** — Editorial/TV appearance photo with network logo overlay.

---

### 7. Quote / Proof Section
Centered H2 heading: "KICK ACID AND TAKE YOUR LIFE BACK". Split layout below: large product lifestyle photo left, pull quote right with label ("STRAIGHT FROM THE SOURCE"), quote text, founder name, title + company.

**Mobile:** Single column. Heading full width, product image full width, quote block full width below.

#### Components
- **Section Heading** — Centered H2.
- **Product Lifestyle Image** — Large square/portrait photo, left column.
- **Pull Quote Block** — Small label ("STRAIGHT FROM THE SOURCE"), quote text in italics, attribution (name, title, company).

---

### 8. Community Section
H2 heading: "WE'VE BUILT A COMMUNITY OF ACID KICKERS". Horizontal carousel of UGC video/photo thumbnails with play button overlays. Pagination dots below.

**Mobile:** Same heading. Single card visible at a time. Left/right arrow navigation replaces pagination dots. Card full viewport width.

#### Components
- **UGC Carousel** — Horizontally scrollable strip of user-generated video/photo cards.
- **Carousel Card** — Thumbnail image, play button overlay.
- **Pagination** — Dot indicators (desktop) / arrow buttons (mobile).

---

### 9. Resources Section
H2 heading: "GET THE MOST FROM ALKAMIND & DR. DARYL". Three-column grid of editorial resource cards, each with a full-bleed image, title overlay, and CTA button.

Cards:
1. Read Off Your Sugar → "SHOP NOW"
2. 1:1 Coaching with Dr. Daryl → "SCHEDULE A CONSULT"
3. The Acid Kicking Academy → "LEARN MORE"

**Mobile:** Horizontally scrollable card strip. ~1.2 cards visible at a time. Cards full height, partial next card hints scroll.

#### Components
- **Resource Card** — Full-bleed image background, title text overlay, CTA pill button at bottom.

---

### 10. UGC / Social Gallery
"@GETOFFYOURACID" heading with social icons right-aligned (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube). Horizontal row of UGC lifestyle photo tiles below.

**Mobile:** Social icons below heading, left-aligned. Photos in 2-column grid.

---

### 11. Footer
Multi-column layout. 4 nav columns (Support, Company, Explore, Reflux News You Can Use). Newsletter column: email input + "SIGN UP" button. "GET OFF YOUR ACID®" large display headline at bottom. Copyright + Privacy Policy + Terms of Use bar below.

**Mobile:** Newsletter signup full width at top of footer. Nav columns collapse to accordions. "GET OFF YOUR ACID®" headline remains large. Legal bar below, stacked.

#### Components
- **Nav Columns** — 4 grouped link lists.
  _Mobile: Each column is an accordion — tap label to expand._
- **Newsletter Signup** — Email input + "SIGN UP" button inline.
  _Mobile: Full-width stacked input + button._
- **Legal Bar** — Copyright year, Privacy Policy, Terms of Use.
  _Mobile: Stacked, centered._
