# Support

Need help with Nazare?

- **Questions:** email **fedor@nazare.engineering**.
- **Bugs & feature requests:** open an [issue](https://github.com/fedorivanenko/nazare/issues/new/choose).
- **Security issues:** see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) — do not use public issues.

Before opening an issue, please check the [README](README.md).

Nazare is in heavy active development; behavior may change between versions. When testing generated themes, keep Theme Check and the Shopify CLI in your workflow.
